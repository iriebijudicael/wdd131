Site Purpose

A job board website is designed to connect job seekers with employers in an efficient, organized, and easy-to-use platform. Its purpose is to provide a central place where employers can post open job positions and where users can search, filter, and apply for those opportunities.

Audience

  • Students & recent graduates: Looking for internships, entry-level jobs, or their first professional opportunity.
  • Experienced professionals: Searching for better positions, career growth, or a job change.
  • Unemployed individuals: Actively looking for immediate job opportunities.
  • Freelancers / part-time workers: Interested in short-term or project-based work.
  • Human Resources departments: Posting job openings internally and externally.
  • Hiring managers: Searching for quality candidates for specific roles.
  • Recruitment agencies: Posting opportunities on behalf of clients.
  • Government programs: Tracking employment trends or promoting job initiatives.
  • Schools & universities: Directing students to employment resources.
